Steps for a berry smoothie:

One thing about me, I sure love smoothies! In fact, I love them so much that they're pretty much all I have for breakfast nowadays. So, in order to make one of my easy & quick to make smoothies, all you'll have to do is follow this recipe!

First, you start out by gathering all the ingredients that I've listed into one convenient place. After that, you can start getting your blender ready for use.

Now you can pour into the blender about 2 dl of oatmilk. The more oatmilk you use, the runnier the texture of the smoothie will be, so add to your liking.

Next, you can add in your berries, a handful or two should get the job done. You can use any berries, it depends on what you personally prefer in this context. I personally like to use a mix of blueberries, strawberries and bananas (yes, I know, they aren't berries), but sometimes I'll switch out the bananas for raspberries!

Next, you're going to want to add in the greek yoghurt. The more you add, the thicker the texture is going to be. I normally don't measure the amount, I just kind of eyeball it, but about 5-6 tablespoons have cut it for me (or 125 grams if you need exact numbers).

After this, you can add about 2-3 tablespoons of honey into the mix, but this is only an estimate, you can add as much as you like, the more you add, the sweeter the smoothie gets!

Now comes the loud part: time to mix! Give the blender a spin, and mix until everything has blended into a fine liquid.

You've successfully made yourself a nice, sweet smoothie! Enjoy!

More below!

You'll need:

  • A Blender
  • 125g of Greek Yoghurt
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of Honey
  • 100-150g of berries of your choice
  • 2dl of Oatmilk
  • A Sweet Tooth
